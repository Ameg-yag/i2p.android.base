How to configure Firefox:

  1. Click HERE, and choose to open the URL in Firefox.
  2. Click the toggle to install the Network Preference add-on.
  3. In the main Firefox menu, click "Tools", then "Add-ons".
  4. Click on "Network Preferences Add-on".
  5. Change the value of "Configure Proxies" to 1.
  6. Change the value of "HTTP Proxy" to 127.0.0.1.
  7. Change the value of "Port" under "HTTP Proxy" to 4444.
  8. Change the value of "HTTPS Proxy" to 127.0.0.1.
  9. Change the value of "Port" under "HTTPS Proxy" to 4444.
  10. You can now browse I2P eepsites!
